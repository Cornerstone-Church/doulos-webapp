  • Travis

    This is Travis Phelix here. I enjoy sports, nature (particularly the water and the sky) and making and building things with my own hands. One of my favorite passages of scripture is the golden rule (Matt 7:12) treat others as you want to be treated. These are words to live by because everyone is made in God's image and we should treat them as such. Growing up I saw this rule put to the test quite often and also broke it myself. I was bullied and in turn bullied others. My mother always told me I have a big heart and I would believe her but also the pain of disappointment would cloud my judgement at times. I can often see Jesus in myself like how I love to marvel at His creation. How I care for others even when I feel so uncared for myself. Or how I like to see things come alive in the essence of carpentry and manual labor. God is in all and above all so I long to see God and love in every one amidst the chaos and heartache. Peace and Love, Travis.
